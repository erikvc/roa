We teach real discipleship to help disciple all towards becoming fully devoted followers of Christ.
We strive to develop mature Christian members and lead them into a deeper commitment and greater service for Christ.

John 4:24
We teach all to worship God in Spirit and in Truth (real worship).

Matthew 28:19-20
We boldly proclaim Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior of the world.
We teach how to be a witness by sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ in the power of the Holy Spirit.
We serve our community by meeting its needs compassionately, humbly, and cheerfully.
